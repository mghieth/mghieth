Working with Scrum: Key Takeaways

While working on agile teams, Scrum played a big role in keeping our projects on track and collaborative. Here’s what stood out to me: 1-Defined Roles: Clear responsibilities improved team coordination. 2-Daily Stand-Ups: Short syncs helped identify blockers early. 3-Sprints: 2-week sprints kept our goals focused and deliverables consistent. 4-Retrospectives: Regular reflection helped us improve our process continuously. Scrum brought structure, flexibility, and transparency to our development cycle — all essential for delivering quality software.
